Zero-Touch Setup

The mod handles ComfyUI launch, Python dependencies, custom node installs, and model lifecycle automatically.

Clean Backgrounds

Lore-Aware Prompts

🎛️

Tunable

Swap checkpoints, LoRAs, and denoise strength from in-game settings. No restarts, no editing files.

🛡️

Crash-Safe

PID-tracked ComfyUI lifecycle with orphan recovery on game load. No hung processes, no leaked VRAM.

Getting Started

Three steps from subscribed to colonists with faces.

1

Point at ComfyUI

Auto-detect or browse to your ComfyUI Portable folder from the mod settings.

2

Grab the Models

Download SDXL base + the RimWorld LoRA. The mod links you to both.

3

Play

New pawns generate on spawn. Existing pawns on map load. Done.

The Personas

Every face a different prompt, every prompt drawn from the pawn it belongs to.

Under the Hood

For the technically curious.

  • SDXL img2img with custom RimWorld LoRA
  • Configurable denoise & sampling strength
  • InSPyReNet dichotomous segmentation
  • Source-alpha floor masking
  • Enclosed hole filling & edge dilation
  • ComfyUI-RMBG auto-installer
  • Harmony-patched auto-generation pipeline
  • Thread-safe state & concurrent downloads
  • Persistent queue across save/load
  • In-game status panel & diagnostics
